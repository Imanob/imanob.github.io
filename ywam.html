Your Words, And Mine

The game was made by five Finnish university students of different backgrounds. We wanted to provide a unique gameplay experience with a beautiful soundtrack and colorful design! We made this prototype as a part of our studies, and thus the game will likely never be finished. In its current state the game has roughly 2/3 of the planned content.

On top of my role as the leader for this project, I acted as one of three programmers, expanding my knowledge in coding in a team environment.

Play the demo

The objective of the game is to communicate with a new people we have never met before. Using its built-in translating skills, our robotic main character sets on a journey to better understand these people and their speech. You can play the demo online here
